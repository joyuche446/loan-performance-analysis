Title:
Loan Performance & Credit Risk Analysis | Excel Dashboard & KPI Insights
Executive Summary:
This project simulates a real-world lending scenario focused on balancing loan growth with credit risk. It analyzes lending performance and borrower behavior using an interactive Excel dashboard designed to provide real-time insights into key KPIs, including loan applications, portfolio quality, and financial performance.
Analysis of 38.6K loans showed 86.2% good loans, generating $65.3M revenue and 13.8% bad loans causing $28.2M losses, highlighting significant risk exposure.
Key next steps include risk-based lending, stricter credit approval, and early default detection to reduce losses and optimize portfolio profitability.
Business Problem:
A financial lending institution is experiencing increasing loan applications but also significant losses from loan defaults. The company lacks visibility into which factors drive loan performance and risk. The objective of this analysis is to evaluate loan performance, identify factors contributing to loan defaults, and provide data-driven insights to improve credit risk management and maximize profitability.
